About Transaction Viewer

The Transaction Viewer is designed to help users track and monitor transactions over a specified period of time. Whether you're interested in following daily, weekly, or monthly transactions, this tool allows you to visualize and manage all your transaction data in one place.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Tracking: Get up-to-the-minute updates on transactions as they occur.
  • Time-Based Filtering: View transactions filtered by specific date ranges—whether it’s daily, weekly, or monthly data.
  • Transaction History: Browse through your transaction history to gain insights over long-term trends.
  • Detailed View: Click on individual transactions to access detailed information, including sender, receiver, and transaction hashes.
  • Customizable Data Export: Export your transaction data for further analysis or record-keeping purposes.

Our goal is to provide a seamless, easy-to-use platform for tracking transactions, empowering users to stay informed about their financial activities over time. Whether you're managing a few transactions or a large dataset, the Transaction Viewer ensures you always have a clear picture of what's happening.
